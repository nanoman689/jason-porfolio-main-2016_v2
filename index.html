Introduction

Hello, my name is Jason, I live near San Jose, California and for the past two years I have been learning front end development and this is my personal site. I have basic knowledge Javascript, CSS, Angular, and HTML. Take a look at myGitHubrepository if you want to see some of the projects I have been working on.

I decided to make a career change a while back and I wanted a new challenge and had been wanting to get back into programming and web production. My personal background is in print production, using Photoshop, Illustrator and InDesign. I have a degree in graphic design and one in film production. I have over a decade of experience in the various fields of graphics and print arts. I'm a animation and visual effects fan at heart and still like to create small projects on the side.
